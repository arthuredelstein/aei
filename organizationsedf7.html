Translations

One of the most important aspects of the work of the Albert Einstein Institution is managing translations of English publications into other languages. We have rigorous translation and evaluation procedures to ensure accurate and careful work. Albert Einstein Institution publications have been translated into more than 40 different languages worldwide.

To see our translations, click on one of the languages below. You may also see a full list of all our translations.
