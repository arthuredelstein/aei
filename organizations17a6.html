Published 1992

"Savarankiska Gynyba Be Bankroto ir Karo." 80 pp. (Translation of "Self-reliant Defense Without Bankruptcy or War") Vilnius: Mintis Publishers.

In this booklet, Sharp discusses the potential of civilian-based defense for the Baltics, East Central Europe, and members of the Commonwealth of Independent States.

Languages available: English, Estonian, Latvian, Lithuanian, Macedonian
