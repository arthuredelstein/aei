De la Dictadura a la Democracia
by Gene Sharp
translated by Caridad Inda



De La Dictadura a la Democracia is the Spanish translation of Gene Sharp's 1993 book, From Dictatorship to Democracy.

Originally published in 1993 in Thailand for distribution among Burmese dissidents, From Dictatorship to Democracy has since spread to several parts of the world. It is a serious introduction to the use of nonviolent action to topple dictatorships. Spanish is one of seventeen different languages that this work appears in (the others are Arabic, Azeri, Belarusian, Burmese, Chin (Burma), Jing-paw (Burma), Karen (Burma), Mon (Burma), Chinese (Simplified Mandarin), Chinese (Traditional Mandarin), English, Farsi, Indonesian, Kyrgyz, Russian, Serbian, Ukrainian).
