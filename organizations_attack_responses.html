Albert Einstein Institution: Responses to Attacks


In 2007, the Albert Einstein Institution and Senior Scholar, Gene Sharp, came under attack by certain writers and in by speeches by President Hugo Chavez of Venezuela, who quoted from an article by a French writer. Some responses to these attacks, as well as the Albert Einstein Institution Consulting Policy, are posted here:
