Policy & Outreach

The Policy and Outreach Program of the Albert Einstein Institution endeavors to provide the best resources to date on strategic nonviolent action to groups fighting for democracy and freedom.

Whether the goal is undermining a dictatorship, blocking a coup d’état, defending against an invasion, or demanding social change, our aim is to help increase the skill, competency, and effectiveness of democratic nonviolent movements to help them achieve liberation.
