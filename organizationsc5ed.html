Waging Nonviolent Struggle: 20th Century Practice and 21st Century Potential
by Gene Sharp



This groundbreaking new work builds on 50 years of Gene Sharp’s academic research and practical experience aiding nonviolent struggles around the world.

It provides unprecedented information about how to strategically plan nonviolent action and make it more effective. Furthermore, it includes twenty-three case accounts of nonviolent struggle in the twentieth century. To see a table of contents and other information, click here.
